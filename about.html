Established in 2003, Hi-Tech Designs has been providing Architectural Design & Drafting services with enviable reputation in residential market in Edmonton, Alberta and beyond. Our cutting-edge creativity combined with deep knowledge & broad experience in the residential home design has made us the most popular choice in Edmonton.

At Hi-Tech Designs we have a team of multicultural designers from different parts of the world with the ability to speak various languages including English, Punjabi, Hindi, Chinese and Pilipino. This has given us the competitive advantage and has resulted in a varied and comprehensive understanding of a wide client base while ensuring a rich company culture. The practice is led by Arvind John who founded the company 13 years ago and is involved daily in its running and its projects.
